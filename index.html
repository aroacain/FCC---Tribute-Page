Lee Hye-ri

Actress, singer, and the nation's little sister

Lee Hye-ri's life and career

  • She was born in Gwanju, Gyeonggi, South Korea on June 9, 1994.
  • In September 2010, Hyeri was announced as a new member of Girl's Day.
  • In 2012, Hyeri made her acting debut in the SBS weekend drama Tasty Life.
  • In 2015, Hyeri was cast as the lead of the tvN drama Reply 1988, one of the highest rated dramas in Korean cable television history.
  • In 2018, Hyeri made her big screen debut as the female lead in Monstrum
  • At 25 years old, Hyeri became the youngest member of UNICEF's Honors Club
